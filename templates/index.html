Welcome to Chlamydomas Genomic Analysis

For several years, green algae have posed a major problem on the French coasts. This project aims to develop a set of tools for the study of this species and to bring together these tools on a website for the benefit of the scientific community. Our website will focus on the genetics of Chlamydomonas, offering several modules to the research community.

  • The visualization of Chlamydomonas genome
  • Phylogenetic study with Newick visualization and phylogenetics trees
  • Sequences alignment
